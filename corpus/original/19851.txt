US copper trims morning losses on Wall St rebound
2009-04-22 10:38:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures erased nearly all of their overnight losses and steadied just below the unchanged level Wednesday morning, as a weaker dollar and a rebound in equities drove the recovery.
