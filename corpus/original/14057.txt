HK, Shanghai shares rebound on Wall St rally
2009-07-14 01:20:00.0
(Reuters) - Hong Kong stocks rebounded from a seven-week low on Tuesday after strong gains on Wall Street spurred bargain-hunting in banks and financials, but traders said the rally could fizzle out as fears about the broader economy weighed.
Shares in Shanghai came close to a 13-month intraday peak struck on Friday, boosted by solid gains in overseas stocks and hopes for an improving earnings outlook.
