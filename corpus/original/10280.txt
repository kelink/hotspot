US copper rallies to 3-wk high on crumbling dollar
2009-10-08 14:13:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures rallied to a three-week high above $2.90 a lb on Thursday, driven by extended losses in the dollar, rallying equities and buying in front of the return of Chinese market participants from week-long holidays.
