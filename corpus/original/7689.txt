April 15 - Treasury report declines to name China a currency manipulator. It says China's yuan continues to be "undervalued" against the dollar, but that Beijing's practices do not fit its legal definition of currency manipulation -- despite what Obama said on the campaign trail. [ID:nWEQ000881]
