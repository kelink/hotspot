Europe stocks lower at midday; financials weigh
2009-05-13 06:59:00.0
(Reuters) - European shares were lower at midday on Wednesday in a choppy session, with financials and miners the biggest fallers and a Bank of England inflation report knocking investor sentiment.
By 1056 GMT, the pan-European FTSEurofirst 300
index of top shares was down 0.8 percent at 845.95 points, having been up as much as 857.12 points earlier in the session.
