US copper steadies with stocks, economic fears cap
2009-07-13 10:47:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures bounced on either side of unchanged Monday morning, tracking volatile swings in equities and held hostage by continued uncertainty about global economic recovery prospects.
