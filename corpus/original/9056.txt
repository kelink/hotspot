US STOCKS-Wall St falters after weak consumer confidence
2009-10-27 10:26:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. stocks fell in choppy trading on Tuesday as a worse-than-expected consumer confidence report overshadowed earlier data that showed home prices rose for the fourth-straight month.
