US copper settles up in dollar-led commods rally
2009-10-12 14:23:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures closed quietly higher on Monday, swept up in a dollar-induced buying spree seen across the broader commodity complex, with supply-side issues in Chile lending some support to the move.
