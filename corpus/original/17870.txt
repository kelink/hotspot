US copper futures surge to new 1-week high at open
2009-05-20 10:43:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures rallied to a new one-week high early Wednesday as technical momentum coupled with a weaker dollar and upbeat equity markets fueled the advance, but poor data from Japan and the United States continued to paint an uncertain economic outlook.
