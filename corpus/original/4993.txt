Factbox: U.S. firms likely to meet China's Hu in Washington
2011-01-14 19:03:00.0
(Reuters) - Several U.S. business leaders will meet with Chinese chief executives in Washington during President Hu Jintao's visit next week, all seeking new deals to tap into economic growth in China.
