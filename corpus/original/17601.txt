UPDATE 1-CNOOC receives Indonesian LNG; starts running Fujian
2009-05-25 03:40:00.0
(Reuters) - China National Offshore Oil Corp (CNOOC) has received its first cargo of liquefied natural gas (LNG) from Indonesia under a long-term contract and is expecting a second cargo before the end of the month.
The cargo, 150,000 cubic metres or 69,000 tonnes, was unloaded from May 15 to 17, CNOOC said in statements on its website (www.cnooc.com.cn).
The chilled gas was sourced from Indonesia's Bontang LNG plant due to delays at the Tangguh project.
