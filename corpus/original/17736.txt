HK shares drop 1.9 pct, Lenovo slides on loss
2009-05-22 01:28:00.0
(Reuters) - Hong Kong shares extended losses to drop 1.9 percent by midday Friday on investor worries about credit rating downgrades for Britain and the United States, deflating hopes for a quick global economic recovery.
Standard & Poor's warned there was a one in three chance Britain could lose its precious triple-A credit rating, stoking fears that the United States, with its increasing budget deficit and weakening economy, may face the same fate.
