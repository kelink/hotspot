CANADA STOCKS-TSX firms as China growth supports resources
2009-07-16 00:03:00.0
(Reuters) - Toronto's main stock index was slightly higher late on Thursday morning as China's strong economic growth in the second quarter boosted the resource-heavy market and offset the drag of lower oil prices on energy shares.
