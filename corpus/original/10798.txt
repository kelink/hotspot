The head the Nordic region's biggest bank said late on Wednesday Nordea has no plans to raise new capital, and will easily meet any new regulatory requirements designed to help ensure banks are in good shape.
Chief Executive Christian Clausen said in a Reuters interview Nordea would be affected by any changes to regulatory requirements, but it was satisfied with its core capital levels.

ELCOTEQ ELQAV.HE The Finnish electronics group, who said on Wednesday it would start talks with a new equity investor from Asia, after negotiations with China's Kaifa Technology had failed, said it was losing business due to a weak balance sheet.
"The balance sheet is in such a weak shape that customers are afraid to give us more business, even if they could," the firm's Chief Executive Jouni Hartikainen was quoted as saying in business daily Kauppalehti.
