US STOCKS-Wall St set to open higher on earnings, dollar drop
2009-11-13 09:20:00.0
(Reuters) - Wall Street was set for a higher open on Friday after solid earnings from Walt Disney Co and JC Penney Co Inc, following the first losing day in seven sessions for blue chips.
The U.S. dollar .DXY, which rallied in the previous session, resumed its decline against other currencies after data showed the U.S. trade deficit widened by the most in more than 10 years, pushing up riskier assets like stocks and commodities.
