Europe shares close lower, up 4.7 pct in August
2009-08-31 11:41:00.0
(Reuters) - European shares closed lower on Monday, dragged down by falls in China and in the United States, where financials slipped after a strong run.
index of top European shares fell 0.6 percent to a provisional close of 972.05 points, after reaching a 10-month high on Friday. The London market was closed for a holiday and will resume trading on Tuesday.
