CANADA STOCKS-TSX falls on oil, but ends August higher
2009-08-31 16:48:00.0
(Reuters) - Toronto's main stock market index fell hard on Monday as oil and other key commodity prices dropped after a sharp selloff in Chinese equities undermined confidence in global economic recovery, but the index still finished August with its sixth straight monthly gain.
The price of oil fell below $70 a barrel, and that pulled the index's big oil and gas group down 2.1 percent. The mining-heavy materials group dropped 2.22 percent.
