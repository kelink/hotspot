UPDATE 1-CDC Corp, CDC Software post quarterly profit; shrs rise
2009-08-17 18:06:00.0
(Reuters) - Chinese software company CDC Corp CHINA.O posted a quarterly profit, helped by lower operating expenses, sending its shares up 9 percent.
The company, which develops enterprise software, online games and Internet portals, posted second-quarter earnings of $3.2 million, or 3 cents a share, attributable to controlling interest, compared with a net loss of $10.0 million, or 9 cents a share, in the year-ago period.
Analysts on average were expecting earnings of 8 cents a share, before items, on revenue of $83.2 million, according to Reuters Estimates.
CDC Corp-affiliate CDC Software CDCS.O, which debuted on Nasdaq early this month, reported earnings of $5.9 million, or 20 cents a share, in its second quarter.
Revenue fell 32 percent, hurt by a fall in licenses and maintenance revenue. [ID:nWNBB6574]
Shares of CDC Corp were up at $2.78, while CDC Software shares were up 18 percent at $10.85 in trading after the bell. (Reporting by Bijoy Koyitty in Bangalore; Editing by Vinu Pilakkott)
