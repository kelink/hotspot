RPT-FACTBOX-Russia's gold mining expansion
2009-08-12 10:49:00.0
(Reuters) - Russia plans to increase its gold output by developing several large deposits in Siberia and the country's far east.
Russia was the world's fifth-largest gold producer in 2008, with a 7.8 percent share of world production. Its reserves are second only to South Africa's.
