China Mobile slips 1.6 pct on slowing user adds
2009-07-20 22:04:00.0
(Reuters) - Shares in the world's largest wireless service provider China Mobile (
) dropped 1.6 percent in early trade on Tuesday, knocking the main index off its firm start, as the company added fewer new users in June amid competitive pressures.
By 0207 GMT, the stock shed 1.6 percent to HK$77.85 while the Hang Seng Index
had fallen 0.3 percent to 19,447.72 after opening 0.5 percent higher.
China Mobile's net subscriber additions in June dropped slightly to 5.01 million from 5.11 million in May.
