UPDATE 1-Goldbrook, Jilin Jien bid for Canadian Royalties
2009-08-07 10:02:00.0
(Reuters) - Canada's Goldbrook Ventures Inc GBK.V and China's Jilin Jien Nickel Industry Co (
The bid represents a 25 percent premium to Canadian Royalties stock's closing price on Thursday.
The total cash consideration to be offered is about C$148.5 million, including accrued interest on debentures, Goldbrook said in a statement.
The companies have formed a new company, Jien Mining Canada Ltd, to make the bid, which is expected to formally begin during the week of Aug. 10. The offer will remain open for at least 35 days, it said.
Goldbrook said Canadian Royalties' Nunavik nickel project presents an excellent development opportunity.
Goldbrook, which is engaged in exploration for Nickel-Platinum-Copper group element sulphide deposits, said they also intend to buy all outstanding 7 percent convertible senior unsecured debentures due March 31, 2015 at C$600 per C$1,000 principal amount of debentures, plus accrued and unpaid interests.
Shares of Goldbrook were up 24 percent at 23.5 Canadian cents on the Toronto Venture Exchange, while Canadian Royalties shares were up 19 percent in morning trade Friday on the Toronto Stock Exchange. (Reporting by Koustav Samanta in Bangalore; Editing by Gopakumar Warrier)
