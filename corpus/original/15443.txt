HK shares rebound 1.3 pct, China stocks lag
2009-06-24 01:26:00.0
(Reuters) - Hong Kong shares rose 1.3 percent on Wednesday, bouncing back from a sharp sell-off previously, but China-listed stocks lagged after regulators approved a second IPO following a near 10-month hiatus in new listings.
