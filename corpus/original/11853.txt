US copper loses with most commodities, dollar firms
2009-08-17 11:42:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures gave up ground in heavy selling on Monday as investors became more wary of risky assets and sold commodities across the board, keying off of sharp declines in oil prices and an increase in the dollar against the euro, traders said.
