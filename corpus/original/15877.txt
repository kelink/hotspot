ADR Report-Foreign stocks slip on economic concerns
2009-06-16 17:29:00.0
(Reuters) - Overseas shares traded in the United States fell on Tuesday, pulled lower by mixed economic U.S. data that stoked worries the economic recovery may take longer than recently anticipated.
New York-traded shares of companies based in emerging markets were hit hardest as some defensive plays, including drugmakers, limited losses from Europe-based companies.
