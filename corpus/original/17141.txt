FOREX-Dollar struggles after sinking to year low vs euro
2009-06-01 11:49:00.0
(Reuters) - The dollar fell to its lowest levels so far this year against both a basket of currencies and the euro on Monday, as optimism that the global economy is on the road to recovery boosted riskier assets.
Commodity-related currencies including the Australian and New Zealand dollars, which are considered to be higher risk, hit eight-month highs against their U.S counterpart as oil prices CLc1 jumped and U.S. stocks rose
