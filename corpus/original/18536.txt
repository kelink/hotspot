ADR Report-ADRs rise after stress test results boost optimism
2009-05-08 14:39:00.0
(Reuters)- Overseas shares traded in the United States rose on Friday after the government's stress test results on big U.S. banks increased optimism over the health of the financial sector, sending share prices higher.

was up 1.7 percent to 923.34. In addition to bank optimism, sentiment in the United States was lifted after data showed that employers shed fewer-than-expected jobs in April. [ID:nN08480299]
