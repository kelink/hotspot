US STOCKS-Weak consumer data saps Wall St gains
2009-08-28 11:22:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. stocks gave up most of their gains on Friday after initially spiking to 10-month highs as weak consumer sentiment data offset an upbeat forecast from chipmaker Intel and better-than-expected profit from computer maker Dell.
