Premium sports car maker Porsche's sales figures are based on the 12 months through January 2009, taken from its financial reports.
Volkswagen sales figures are from the company's 2008 annual report.
* VW posted annual sales of 113.8 billion euros ($151.2 billion), more than 16 times Porsche's approximately 7 billion euros.
* VW sold 6.27 million cars last year, more than 70 times the 87,000 cars delivered by Porsche.
* VW has 370,000 staff globally, almost 30 times Porsche's 12,800 employees.
* VW's market value stands at 73.8 billion euros, compared with Porsche's 4.9 billion euros.
* Porsche's stable of models comprise the 911 sports coupe, the Boxster cabrio and its hard-top derivative Cayman, the SUV Cayenne and the luxury sedan Panamera, with market launch slated for January 2010.
* Volkswagen's brands comprise the namesake VW hallmark on passenger cars, vans and light trucks, Audi, Skoda, Seat, Bentley luxury cars, sports cars Lamborghini and Bugatti as well as Scania trucks.
