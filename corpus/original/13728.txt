TAIPEI, July 21
-- Taiwan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs said it would consider taking legal action against a former Taiwan representative to Switzerland if it is proven that the former diplomat's memoirs revealed secrets.
-- Taiwan's minister of economic affairs will meet his Chinese counterpart at the APEC meeting in Singapore to discuss a possible two-way Economic Cooperation Framework Agreement (ECFA).
-- An Economic Cooperation Framework Agreement between Taiwan and China would hurt more than 3,500 enterprises and cause nearly T$100 billion (3 billion) in annual losses, according to a research report by Taiwan's Ministry of Economic Affairs.
-- Taiwan's Industrial Development Bureau will announce its plan on Wednesday for subsidies to domestic DRAM makers.
-- Officials seek to expand the total area for Taiwan's organic agriculture to 5,000 ha by the end of 2012 and raise the sector's annual production value to more than T$3 billion.
