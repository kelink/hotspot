China PICC gets Hong Kong exchange nod for up to $3 billion IPO: source
2012-06-22 04:30:00.0
(Reuters) - China's state-owned insurer PICC Group has secured approval from the Hong Kong stock exchange for a planned initial public offering of up to $3 billion, though volatile markets mean a sale is unlikely to take place anytime soon, a source with direct knowledge of the matter said.
