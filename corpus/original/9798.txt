US copper slips at open as economic concerns weigh
2009-10-15 10:53:00.0
(Reuters) - U.S. copper futures steadied at slightly lower levels Thursday morning, weighed down by a stabilizing dollar and softer tone in equities that reflected concerns about the pace of economic recovery.
