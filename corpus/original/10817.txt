MANILA, Sept 1
- President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo and her husband have nothing more to say on the cancelled telecommunications deal with China's ZTE Corp, the palace said, as the Senate was set to resume hearings into allegations of kickbacks in the transaction (ALL PAPERS)
- Defence Secretary Gilberto Teodoro has said the Philippines' annual defence budget should be on par with Thailand at $3.4 billion from the current $1.2 billion (PHILIPPINE DAILY INQUIRER)
- Senator Benigno "Noynoy" Aquino, son of former President Cory Aquino, has said he is "very, very close" to making a decision on whether to run for president in the 2010 elections (PHILIPPINE STAR)
- The government is reviewing full-year growth forecasts following the better-than-expected performance of the economy in the second quarter, officials said (BUSINESSWORLD)
- The Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) has agreed to lower the guarantee fee on Samurai bonds proposed to be issued by the government to pre-fund its needs for next year, Finance Secretary Margarito Teves has said (PHILIPPINE STAR)
