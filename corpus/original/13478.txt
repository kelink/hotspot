-- A National Bureau of Statistics official said China has the capability to collect economic data for the first half in 15 days and ensure accuracy, rebuffing a report by Heritage Foundation, a Washington-based think tank, which questioned the reliability of Chinese data.
-- China's Ministry of National Defense will launch an official website in both Chinese and English on Aug. 1 to allow the public more access to the information about the People's Liberation Army.
-- The Shanghai municipal government plans to allow its district-level authorities the power to approve foreign investment projects of up to $100 million from Aug. 1, from the previous threshold of $30 million.
