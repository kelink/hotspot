Here are some details about the spread of the new H1N1 flu, a mixture of swine, bird and human viruses, which has killed over 140 people and infected nearly 29,000, according to the World Health Organisation's latest tallies.
CONFIRMED CASES: The number of confirmed cases of H1N1 is now at least 28,799 in 74 countries. The World Health Organisation includes Taiwan and Hong Kong in China for its country tally.
