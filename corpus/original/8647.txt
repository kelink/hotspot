GLOBAL MARKETS-Crude climbs on economic data. stocks falter
2009-11-02 13:43:00.0
(Reuters) - Crude oil rebounded on Monday as strong factory activity worldwide and a surprise rise in U.S. pending home sales boosted risk appetite after last week's sell-off, but stocks faltered on fears the economic recovery is still on shaky ground.
