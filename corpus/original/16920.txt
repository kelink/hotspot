FACTBOX-Worldwide spread of flu outbreak
2009-06-03 07:42:00.0
(Reuters) - Saudi Arabia has confirmed its first case of the H1N1 flu virus in a Filipina nurse, bringing the number of confirmed cases to 20,000 in nearly 70 countries.
Chile said on Tuesday that a Chilean man had died from H1N1 flu, the country's first death from the new virus. Luxembourg confirmed its first case of H1N1 flu on Tuesday.
CONFIRMED CASES: The number of confirmed cases of H1N1 is 20,000 in 68 countries. The World Health Organisation includes Taiwan and Hong Kong in China for its country tally.
